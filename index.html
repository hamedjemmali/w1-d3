Focaccia with caramelized Onions ,
Caranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appestizer topped with sweet caramelized onions , tart cranberries , and cremy brie if will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 12/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil,plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions , thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vingar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil,for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie , cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paringknife if desired , though it's not necessary )
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

in a medium-sized mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment , mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absord . you don't need to actually knead the dough,though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour ..
